# Etsy SEO, Pricing & Photography

Write Etsy listing copy the way the live Etsy lane does it: searched words first,
then the click.

## Titles + tags
- Front-load the title with the exact multi-word phrase a buyer would type.
- Use all 13 tags.
- Tags should be multi-word phrases, not wasted single words.
- Avoid duplicate exact words across tags when possible; cover both head and
  long-tail intent.
- Use attributes like room, orientation, or style when they help search intent.

## Pricing
- Price against the top-seller band for the query, not by guessing cheap.
- Sets and bundles generally out-earn singles.
- Intro-sale logic beats permanent underpricing.

## Imagery / click angle
The first image does most of the clicking. Lead with a believable styled-room angle,
show the art large, and make the click promise obvious. Trust comes from clarity,
not decorative fluff.

## Deliverable bar
A strong listing pack gives a searchable title, 13 useful tags, a realistic price,
and a hero angle that would earn the click in Etsy's grid.
