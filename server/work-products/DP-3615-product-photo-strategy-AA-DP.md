## Key Research Findings (Etsy/Pinterest Benchmarks)
Sources: Etsy market listings for square digital botanical prints, gallery wall sets, vintage/herbarium styles; digital planner listing mockup tutorials; Marmalead/Etsy seller handbook on photography; product background guides.

- **First-image grid CTR winners**:
  - Square 1:1 aspect (recommended 2600–3000 px on short side) dominates thumbnails.
  - Clean white/light herbarium or desk surfaces with high contrast (gold leaf accents for AA; hot-pink/black for DP).
  - Single dominant subject + subtle context (plant on desk for AA; open planner page visible for DP).
  - Avoid busy backgrounds or fake species confusion.

- **Trust tiles**:
  - Real product shots (actual printed PDF on paper, not substitute docs).
  - Inclusion/size tiles showing multiple sizes or full set in one frame.
  - Lifestyle context: credible home/desk settings, no generic planner sludge.

- **Mobile readability & brand consistency**:
  - High-contrast text overlays minimal; rely on image itself.
  - Consistent lighting (soft natural or studio) and angle (straight-on or slight 3/4 for depth).
  - Sets of 6–9 images sell best for gallery walls; singles for hero shots.

- **Stock/photo scene types that convert**:
  - Botanical: real aroid/monstera/philodendron on white paper, pressed herbarium style, gold-leaf watermark, plant on minimalist desk or windowsill.
  - Digital planner: actual PDF pages visible on tablet or printed on white paper, workflow desk with near-black/hot-pink accents, clean adult productivity aesthetic, buyer-visible PDF files.

- **Exclusions (misleading/off-brand)**:
  - Fake species, AI-generated plants that don't match real aroids, unrelated documents, generic stock planner sludge, phone/lifestyle unrelated to productivity.

## Stock/Background Sourcing Rules
- Primary: high-resolution white paper scans or real photographed surfaces (herbarium paper for AA, crisp printer paper for DP).
- Secondary: real desk/home environments with natural light (no heavy filters).
- Gold leaf/watermark assets: vector or photographed metallic leaf applied in post (AA only).
- Hot-pink/black accents: brand color swatches only; no unrelated props.
- All stock must be original or licensed; no public-domain botanical illustrations that risk species confusion.

## Visual-Truth Rules
- Every plant shown must be a real, identifiable aroid species (AA).
- Every planner page must show actual content from the DP product (no placeholder sludge).
- No AI-generated fake plants or documents.
- Printed product must be photographed or realistically mocked from real files.
- Buyer-visible PDF edges/files required in lifestyle shots.

## Prompt/Style Recipes for Designer-Media
Base style: photorealistic product photography, soft natural or studio lighting, 3000 px square, high detail, commercial stock aesthetic.

**AA recipe**:
"Photorealistic square product photo of [specific real aroid species] leaf on crisp white herbarium paper, subtle gold leaf botanical mark in corner, soft natural window light, minimalist desk or flat surface, high detail, editorial botanical style, no text, square 1:1, 3000px --ar 1:1 --stylize 250"

**DP recipe**:
"Photorealistic square product photo of open [planner name] page on white printer paper with near-black and hot-pink accent elements visible, actual PDF file edge visible in scene, clean adult productivity desk setting, soft lighting, premium printable aesthetic, no unrelated objects, square 1:1, 3000px --ar 1:1 --stylize 200"

## Negative Prompts / Do-Not-Use List
- Fake/AI plants, non-aroid species, cartoonish or watercolor unless specified
- Generic planner templates, unrelated notebooks/phones, busy backgrounds
- Text overlays, watermarks (except AA gold leaf), low-res or blurry elements
- Lifestyle unrelated to desk/productivity or home gallery wall
- Substitute documents, stock photo people, cluttered scenes
- Any scene that could be mistaken for physical product instead of digital/printable

## QA Checklist (before any shot is approved)
- [ ] Square 1:1 ratio, ≥2600 px short side
- [ ] Real product or accurate mock from actual files visible
- [ ] Brand color accents only (gold for AA; black/hot-pink for DP)
- [ ] No fake species or placeholder content
- [ ] Ties directly to grid CTR, trust, inclusion, or lifestyle
- [ ] Mobile-readable contrast and composition
- [ ] Consistent lighting/angle across set
- [ ] Visual-truth verified against product files
